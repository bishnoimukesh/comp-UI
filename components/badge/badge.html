Badges

Badges scale to match the size of the immediate parent element by using relative font sizing and em units. Badges no longer have focus or hover styles for links.

Avatars with status

Avatars badge gives the presence of the user. There are three indicators for the user presence use: .avatar status online, offline, and do not disturb.

Icons with badge

Icons with badge gives the notification related to that icon. To use icon badge use .icon-badge .
